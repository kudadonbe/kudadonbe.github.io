Who?!

I'm Kudadonbe — Hussain Shareef on paper. I study Information and Communication Technology in the Maldives.

Most of my time is spent in conversation: thinking through ideas, sitting with people who build things, and figuring out what's worth making. This site collects what comes out of that.

I'm drawn to two kinds of problems — things that already exist but could work better, and things that don't exist yet but should. Both keep me busy.

I also care about Dhivehi writing and literature. I've been learning the craft of poetry and have started writing — poems for now, but essays, stories, and the occasional argument will find their way here too.
