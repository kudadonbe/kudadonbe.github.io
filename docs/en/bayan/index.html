بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم

السلام عليكم ورحمة الله وبركاته

The life of this world is brief. Very few manage to spend it fully. So I resolve to make the most of what remains — and that depends on the goodwill and support of those around me. My days have been lived among you, inside what you own and tend. It is likely that, through carelessness or weakness of faith, I have at some point trespassed on your rights. That you may not have noticed does not mean it did not happen. It did — without my having thought carefully enough.

So I ask your forgiveness for any harm that came from me. For whatever wrong I caused by transgressing against what was yours — may Allah return it to you doubled. May He bless your wealth, lengthen your years, and open the way for more good deeds. Allah is capable of all things.
