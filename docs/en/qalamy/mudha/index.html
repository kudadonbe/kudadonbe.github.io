Wealth

Wealth belongs to Allah, the Exalted. Whatever passes through our hands is only a trust. It ought to be earned by the means He has permitted, and spent in the ways He has permitted. On the Day of Reckoning, this too will be asked about.

There is no real good in economic principles built by those who do not accept the Creator as the one true God. An economy should be ordered according to the command of the One to whom belong the heavens and the earth, and everything between them.
